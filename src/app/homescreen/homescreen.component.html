Model United Nations at the University of Pittsburgh

Welcome to the offical website for the PittMUN team! Model United Nations provides students the opportunity to learn about and discuss today's most relevant issues of international diplomacy through participation in an academic simulation. Pitt Model United Nations is a long-standing club at the University of Pittsburgh. The club prepares and competes in Model United Nations conferences throughout the year.

Highschool Conference
Registration for this year's high school Model UN conference occurs annually in the fall. We hope to see you soon!
Weekly Meetings
PittMUN holds weekly meetings on Monday's at 9:00pm. Meeting are held in Posvar in room 4130. *During the COVID-19 Pandemic meetings are held on zoom.
Socials
We hold bi-weekly socials for members. Past socials include game night, movie night, bowling, and casual hangouts.
