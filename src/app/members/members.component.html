Head Delegate:
The Head Delegate is in charge of training all members.
The Head Delegate also plans and runs our in-house training conferences. In addition
to registering for conferences, the Head Delegate represents the team
at the Head Delegate meetings while at conferences. The goal of
the Head Delegate is to provide the necessary
support for the team to be successful at conferences.

Director of Finance:
The Director of Finance is responsible for preparing the annual budget
and supplemental budget requests. Additionally, the Director of Finance
is responsible for processing all club business involving budget items,
such as business travel advances and disbursements. The Director of Finance
is responsible for attending appropriate budget hearings
related to club finances.

The Secretary General of the high school conference is responsible for
working with the University Center for International Studies (UCIS)
